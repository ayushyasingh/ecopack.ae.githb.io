Director's Message

  1. Home
  2. Ecopack@Glance
  3. Director's Message

If you look through a tube or a core, you see a closed circle, which summarises a cardboard tube in its simplest form.

Ecopack will do more than create closed circles. With our eyes open to the world we are ever striving to innovate and produce made-to-measure solutions to the problems of every customer.

Ecopack will continue to expand on its leading position in the cardboard tubes, cores and edge protectors sector by means of a customer-oriented approach based on continuous optimisation of our service and our production processes, while respecting human values and the environment.

Working closely with our customers, our open minded approach and technical ability helps us to offer solutions to specific problems.
