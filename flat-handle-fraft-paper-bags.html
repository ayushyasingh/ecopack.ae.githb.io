Flat Handle Paper Bags

These bags offer great value for money as they are machine made. Flat handle bags are available in white or natural brown Kraft paper.

We offer a range of standard sizes which can be overprinted or fully manufactured.

This style of bag is most commonly used in the food to go market, but can also make a more cost effective promotional bag.

Small Quantities – 100 to 3000 Bags

We offer a digital overprint service on our Flat handle bags. This is ideal for small quantities and quick delivery. We can print as little as 100 bags and offer an express printing service.

Fully Manufactured Flexo Printed Bags – 10000 Bags Upwards

The minimum order quantity for fully manufactured bags starts from 10,000. We offer 4 popular sizes as standard, but can also offer other sizes if required.

There are no restrictions to the print area with fully manufactured bags.

We have to make printing plates, so on your first order there will be an origination cost.

Flat Handle Kraft Paper bags with 4 Colour Process Printing

We can offer digitally printed bags with full colour process printing. This process is suitable for smaller quantities and there are some restrictions to the print area on the bag.
