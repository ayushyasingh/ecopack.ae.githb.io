Twisted Handle Kraft Paper Bags

These carrier bags offer great value for money as they are machine made. Twisted handle bags are available in white or natural brown Kraft paper.

We offer a range of standard sizes from small A5 size gift bags to large retail bags. These bags are very popular with boutiques and used widely in the promotional market.

Small Quantities – 100 to 3000 Bags

We offer a digital overprint service on our Kraft twisted handle bags. This is ideal for small quantities and quick delivery. We can print as little as 100 bags and offer an express printing service.

We can over print a full range of sizes from single to full colour artwork and there are no set up cost!

Fully Manufactured Flexo Printed Bags – 1000 Bags Upwards

The minimum order quantity for fully manufactured bags starts from 1000. We offer 3 popular sizes with this low minimum order quantity.

Other sizes are available but generally the minimum order quantity would be 3000 bags. There are no restrictions to the print area with fully manufactured bags.

We have to make printing plates, so on your first order there will be an origination cost.

Twisted Handle Kraft Paper bags with 4 Colour Process Printing

We can offer digitally printed carrier bags with full colour process printing. This process is suitable for smaller quantities and there are some restrictions to the print area on the bag.

For larger quantities or for full print coverage we also Litho print this style of bag.

Please speak to one of our dedicated sales team who will guide through the different options and processes available. They will be able to provide you with all the information you need to find the right paper carrier bags for your needs.
